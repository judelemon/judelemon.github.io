About Us

The Philosophy of the school

Leith school of music was established in 2014, the aim was to create a music school with affordable and competitive rates for all ages to learn guitar, or to hone their skills further.

The Studio

Leith school of Music is located in the heart of Leith, at Leith Business Centre, 130 Leith Walk. If it's advanced electric lead guitar or just that song you've always wanted to learn, then there is no better place equipped and priced than Leith school of music to learn or improve your ability.

The Teacher

Experienced guitar tutor Daniel Barr has been building up the good reputation of the school for over three years now. All classes and lessons are taught in a relaxed, friendly and professional environment.
